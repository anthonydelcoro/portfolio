**Description:** Powdered materials is selectively consolidated by melting it together 
using a heat source such as a laser or electron beam. The powder surrounding the 
consolidated part acts as support material for overhanging features.

**Alternative Names:**
- SLS™- Selective Laser Sintering; DMLS™-
- Direct Metal Laser Sintering; SLM™- Selective
- Laser Melting: EBM™- Electron Beam Melting;
- SHS™- Selective Heat Sintering;
- MJF™- Multi-Jet Fusion

**Strengths:**
- High level of complexity
- Powder acts as support material
- Wide range of materials

**Materials:**
- Plastics
- Metal
- Ceramic Powders
- Sand
