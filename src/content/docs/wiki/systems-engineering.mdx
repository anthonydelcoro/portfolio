title: Systems Engineering
description: Notes on Systems Engineering
---

Systems engineering is a structured process used to guide a system from an initial idea or need all the 
way through design, development, testing, operation, and retirement. At a general level, it is meant to 
make sure that complex systems are not designed in pieces without coordination. Instead, it connects 
requirements, design decisions, interfaces, risk management, testing, and reviews into one organized 
process. NASA provides a helpful reference because its systems engineering framework clearly shows how 
the process moves from concept to operation and how formal reviews are used along the way.
