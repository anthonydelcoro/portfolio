### Setting ANSYS Mechanical to dark mode:

1. Click “File” in the top left corner.
2. Navigate to “Options…”
3. Expand the “Mechanical” tab and click on “UI Options”
4. Under “Style” change the Color Theme to Dark

### Setting a section view in ANSYS

1. In the “Results” tab, select the Section Pane tool and draw a line along the section you want to cut. 
2. You can modify the view in the left hand menu by selecting the section plane and using the tools at the top of the menu.
