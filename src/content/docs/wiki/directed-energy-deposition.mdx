**Description:** Powder or wire is fed into a melt pool which
has been generated on the surface of the part where it adheres to the underlying part or layers
by using an energy source such as a laser or electron beam.This is essentially a form of
automated build-up welding.

**Alternative Names:**
- LMD - Laser Metal Deposition
- LENS™- Laser Engineered Net Shaping
- DMD™- Direct Metal Deposition (DM3D)
- LENS™ – Laser Engineered Net Shaping
- DMD™ – Direct Metal Deposition DM3D, 

**Strengths:**
-  Not limited by direction or axis
- Effective for repairs and adding features
- Multiple materials in a single part
- Highest single-point deposition rates

**Materials:**
- Metal Wire and Powder, with Ceramics
