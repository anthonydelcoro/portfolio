**Description:** Liquid bonding agents are selectively applied onto thin layers of powdered material to build up
parts layer by layer. The binders include organic and inorganic materials. Metal or ceramic
powdered parts are typically fired in a furnace after they are printed. 

**Alternative Names:**
- 3DP™- 3D Printing
- ExOne
- Voxeljet

**Strengths:**
- High level of complexity
- Powder acts as support material
- Wide range of materials

**Materials:**
- Powdered Plastic
- Metal
- Ceramics
- Glass
- Sand
