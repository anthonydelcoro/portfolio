**Description:** A vat of liquid photopolymer resin is cured through selective exposure to light (via a laser
or projector) which then initiates polymerization and converts the exposed areas to a solid part.

**Alternative Names:**
- SLA™- Stereolithography Apparatus
- DLP™- Digital Light Processing
- 3SP™- Scan, Spin, and Selectively Photocure
- CLIP™ – Continuous Liquid Interface Production

**Strengths:**
- High level of accuracy and complexity
- Smooth surface finish
- Accommodates large build areas

**Materials:**
- UV-Curable Photopolymer Resins
