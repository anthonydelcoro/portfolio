**Description:** Material is extruded through a nozzle or orifice
in tracks or beads, which are then combined into multi-layer models. Common varieties include
heated thermoplastic extrusion (similar to a hot glue gun) and syringe dispensing. 

**Alternative Names:**
- FFF - Fused Filament Fabrication
- FDM™- Fused Deposition Modeling

**Strengths:**
- Inexpensive and economical
- Allows for multiple colors
- Can be used in an office environment
- Parts have good structural properties

**Materials:**
- Thermoplastic Filaments and Pellets (FFF)
- Liquids, and Slurries (Syringe Types)
